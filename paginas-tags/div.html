16 - HTML Div

16.1 - Divisão HTML

O elemento <div> é por padrão um elemento de bloco, o que significa que ocupa toda a largura disponível e vem com quebras de linha antes e depois. Ele ocupa toda a largura disponível, também não possui atributos obrigatórios, mas style, classe idsão comuns.

Código:

Lorota <div style="background-color: burlywood;">I am a div</div> mais lorota.

Exemplo:

Lorota
Eu sou uma DIV
mais lorota.

16.2 - Div como um contêiner

O elemento <div> é frequentemente usado para agrupar seções de uma página da web.

Código:

<div style="background-color: burlywood;">
<h2>London</h2>
<p>London is the capital city of England.</p>
<p>London has over 13 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: rgb(161, 154, 145);">
<h2>Oslo</h2>
<p>Oslo is the capital city of Norway.</p>
<p>Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: #454545;">
<h2>Rome</h2>
<p>Rome is the capital city of Italy.</p>
<p>Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

Exemplo:

London

London is the capital city of England.

London has over 13 million inhabitants.

Oslo

Oslo is the capital city of Norway.

Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.

Rome

Rome is the capital city of Italy.

Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.


16.2 - Div Lado a Lado - Float

É possivel colocar um div ao lado da outra, utilizando o atributo float do CSS.

Código:

<div style="background-color: burlywood; width:33%; float:left;">
<h2>London</h2>
<p>London is the capital city of England.</p>
<p>London has over 13 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: rgb(161, 154, 145); width:33%; float:left;">
<h2>Oslo</h2>
<p>Oslo is the capital city of Norway.</p>
<p>Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: #454545; width:33%; float:left;">
<h2>Rome</h2>
<p>Rome is the capital city of Italy.</p>
<p>Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

Exemplo:

London

London is the capital city of England.

London has over 13 million inhabitants.

Oslo

Oslo is the capital city of Norway.

Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.

Rome

Rome is the capital city of Italy.

Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.


16.3 - Div Lado a Lado - Inline-block

Também é possivel colocar um div ao lado da outra, utilizando o atributo inline-block do CSS.

Código:

<div style="background-color: burlywood; width: 30%; display: inline-block;">
<h2>London</h2>
<p>London is the capital city of England.</p>
<p>London has over 13 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: rgb(161, 154, 145); width: 30%; display: inline-block;">
<h2>Oslo</h2>
<p>Oslo is the capital city of Norway.</p>
<p>Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.</p>
</div>

<div style="background-color: #454545; width: 30%; display: inline-block;">
<h2>Rome</h2>
<p>Rome is the capital city of Italy.</p>
<p>Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.</p>
</div>

Exemplo:

London

London is the capital city of England.

London has over 13 million inhabitants.

Oslo

Oslo is the capital city of Norway.

Oslo has over 600.000 inhabitants.

Rome

Rome is the capital city of Italy.

Rome has almost 3 million inhabitants.
